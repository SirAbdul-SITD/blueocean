Products Overview
Solar Panels

Our high-efficiency solar panels are engineered to harness the sun’s energy, providing a clean and renewable power source for homes, businesses, and industrial applications. These panels are designed to withstand various weather conditions, ensuring long-term performance and durability, contributing to a sustainable future.

Solar Charge Controllers

Our advanced solar charge controllers play a crucial role in optimizing your solar power system. By regulating the flow of electricity from the solar panels to the batteries, they prevent overcharging and prolong battery life, ensuring your energy storage is both safe and efficient.

Solar Power Inverters

Blue Oceans offers reliable solar power inverters that convert direct current (DC) from solar panels into alternating current (AC) used by most household and industrial appliances. These inverters are essential for integrating solar power into your electrical systems, offering robust performance and compatibility with various power needs.

Dry Cell (Gel) Batteries

Designed for longevity and reliability, our dry cell gel batteries provide excellent energy storage solutions for solar systems. These maintenance-free batteries are spill-proof, making them ideal for off-grid applications and backup power systems, offering stable power with minimal upkeep. Plus, they come with a 12-month warranty for added peace of mind.

Lithium-Ion Batteries

Our lithium-ion batteries are at the forefront of energy storage technology, offering superior capacity and efficiency. With rapid charging capabilities and a longer cycle life, these batteries are perfect for high-demand solar applications, ensuring you have consistent and reliable power whenever you need it. Additionally, they come with a 5-year warranty, providing you with long-term confidence in your energy solution.

Solar Powered Street Lights

Our solar-powered street lights are an environmentally friendly solution for outdoor lighting needs. They operate completely off-grid, using solar energy stored during the day to illuminate streets, parks, and pathways at night. With minimal maintenance required, they offer a cost-effective and sustainable lighting option.

Water Pumping Machine (Booster Pump)

Ideal for agriculture and water management, our solar pumping machines are designed to efficiently increase water pressure using solar energy. These booster pumps are perfect for irrigation systems and domestic water supply, reducing reliance on traditional power sources and lowering operational costs.

Solar Powered Submersible Pumps

Our solar-powered submersible pumps provide a reliable water pumping solution for deep wells and boreholes. Powered entirely by solar energy, these pumps are perfect for remote locations, offering a sustainable method to access groundwater for agricultural, domestic, or industrial use.

Ceiling Fans

Our energy-efficient ceiling fans are designed to operate on low power, making them ideal for use with solar power systems. They provide effective cooling with minimal energy consumption, enhancing comfort in residential and commercial spaces while contributing to reduced energy bills.

Standing Fans

Our range of standing fans offers flexible cooling solutions with portability and efficiency. These fans are designed for maximum air circulation with low energy usage, making them perfect for homes, offices, and any indoor environment where reliable and cost-effective ventilation is needed.

Orbit Fans

Our Orbit Fans are high-performance, energy-efficient ceiling fans designed for both solar and traditional power use. These fans are engineered to deliver optimal airflow while consuming significantly less energy than conventional models.

Solar Kits

Our Solar Kits are comprehensive solutions designed to make solar energy accessible and user-friendly for a wide range of applications. These kits provide everything needed for efficient solar power generation, enabling users to harness renewable energy with ease.
